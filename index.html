⛵   The Great Voyage  ·  1895  ·  Jelsa, Norway → Wisconsin & Iowa, U.S.A.
Thomas Landsness Born Norway, 1871 — departed for America in 1895, settling in Wisconsin. He lived until 1953.

Bertha Johannesdatter Born Jelsa, Rogaland, 1880 — crossed the Atlantic that same year, 1895. She lived until 1959.

Together they planted Norwegian roots that have grown into six generations of American family.
